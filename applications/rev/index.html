About Us

The Ekiti State College of Health Science and Technology, Ijero-Ekiti was established in 2010 by the Ekiti State Government. It is reputed for high academic standards and professional training. This is a tertiary educational institution that offers a Higher National Diploma, National Diploma, Professional Diploma, and Certificate Programme

Online Application for 2021/2022 Admission Details

The Ekiti State College of Health Science and technology, Ijero-Ekiti, Ekiti State, is Inviting suitable and qualified Candidate for Admission into Full-Time and Part-Time program into the College of Health Science and Technology, IJERO-EKIT, Ekiti State, Nigeria. Addmission are also available into various departments as follows:
DEPARTMENT OF LABORATORY SCIENCE (LSC)
THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH SCIENCE(EHS)
THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH INFORMATION MANAGEMENT (HIMGT)
DEPARTMENT OF COMMUNITY HEALLTH (CH)
DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC HEALTH NURSING (PHN)
BASIC MEDICAL SCIENCE (BMSC)
DEPARTMENT OF DISPENSING OPTICIAN (DOP)

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS:

Candidates should possess five credit passes in WASC, NECO, GCE or equivalent at not more than two sittings in relevant subjects from: ENGLISH LANGUAGE
BIOLOGY/AGRIC. SCIENCE

APPLICATION REGISTRATION PROCEDURE

ATTENTION: DO NOT make your payments on www.remita.net (Remita Website) GENERATE YOUR payments INVOICE ONLY ON THIS WEBSITE (Through your Dashboard)
New Applicant should click on "Create Account Here" and select the programme (e.g Full-Time / Part-Time Diploma, and Higher National Diploma in any of the Departments) and fill other details to create an account.
Login into your account with your Username and Password. Click on make Payment to generate application invoice with whichwith Your DEBIT/ATM Card.
Candidates are to pay application fee of Seven thousand Five Hundred naira (N10,500.00) excluding bank charges on Remita from EKiti State College of Health Science And Technology, IJERO-EKITI, EKITI State Website)
Login to your account on-line after payment to confirm your payment and complete the on-line Full-Time/Part-Time application form.
Submit the Online application form and print out a copy of the Screening Schedule slip.
Students who are able to see the option (Print Admission Letter) on their profile can go ahead and pay their Accptance Fees using the same process of application fee payment through Remita by generating School fee invoice number on their profiles and confirm payment after they have paid through the remita gateway from the site. Application closes very soon for both Full-Time and Part-Time Candidates.
